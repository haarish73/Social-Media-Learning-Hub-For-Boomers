Getting Started with Twitter

Follow these steps to start using Twitter:

  1. Download the Twitter app from your app store.
  2. Sign up with your email or phone number.
  3. Create a username (handle) and password.
  4. Set up your profile by adding a photo and bio.
  5. Follow other accounts and explore tweets!

Intermediate Twitter Features

Take your Twitter experience to the next level with these features:

  • Retweets: Share tweets from others with your followers.
  • Hashtags: Use hashtags to make your tweets discoverable to a wider audience.
  • Mentions: Tag others in your tweets using the @ symbol.
  • Lists: Organize people you follow into custom lists.
  • Trending Topics: Explore popular conversations in real-time.

Advanced Twitter Techniques

Learn how to become a pro at using Twitter:

  • Analytics: Use Twitter Analytics to track engagement and performance of your tweets.
  • Twitter Ads: Promote your tweets to a wider audience with paid promotions.
  • Advanced Search: Use advanced search filters to find tweets and users with precision.
  • Pinned Tweets: Pin important tweets to the top of your profile.
  • Twitter Spaces: Host live audio conversations with your followers.
