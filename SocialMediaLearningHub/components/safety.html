Workshop Overview

Our Safety Workshop covers the importance of personal safety, workplace safety, and emergency procedures. Experts will guide you through various scenarios and provide practical tips to stay safe in different environments.

Understand workplace hazards and how to minimize risks in a work environment.

Time: 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Session 3: Emergency Procedures

Learn the key steps to take in an emergency situation, including evacuation and first aid.

Time: 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Safety Tips

  • Always stay aware of your surroundings.
  • Practice emergency drills regularly.
  • Keep first aid kits accessible and up to date.
  • Know how to report hazards or unsafe conditions.
  • Follow safety guidelines provided by experts or authorities.
