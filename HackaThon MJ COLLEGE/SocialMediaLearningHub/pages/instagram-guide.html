Getting Started with Instagram

Follow these steps to start using Instagram:

  1. Download the Instagram app from your app store.
  2. Sign up with your email or phone number.
  3. Create a username and password.
  4. Set up your profile by adding a photo and bio.
  5. Follow people and explore content!

Intermediate Instagram Features

Take your Instagram experience to the next level with these features:

  • Stories: Share photos and videos that disappear after 24 hours.
  • Reels: Create and explore short, engaging videos.
  • Hashtags: Use hashtags to reach a wider audience.
  • Direct Messaging: Connect privately with friends and followers.
  • Explore Page: Discover new content tailored to your interests.

Advanced Instagram Techniques
